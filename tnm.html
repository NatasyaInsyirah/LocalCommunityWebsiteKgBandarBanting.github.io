TAMAN FERTIGASI

This Fertigation project was supported by JABATAN PERTANIAN NEGERI SELANGOR and Land area is 1.4 HA and 1.2 HA


Benifits of fertigation such as:

Fertigation provides essential nutrients directly to the root zone of plants, ensuring they have access to the right amount of nutrients at the right time.
This helps to promote healthy plant growth, increase yields, and improve overall crop quality.

Fertigation is an efficient method of fertilizer application as it delivers nutrients directly to the root zone. This reduces nutrient wastage and minimizes the risk of nutrient leaching,
where fertilizers get washed away from the root zone by excessive irrigation or rainfall.

Fertigation allows for precise control over nutrient application, making it easier to tailor the fertilization regime to meet the specific needs of different crops or
even different growth stages of the same crop

Fertigation is an essential component of hydroponic systems and soilless culture, where plants grow without soil
and rely on nutrient-rich water solutions for their growth.
