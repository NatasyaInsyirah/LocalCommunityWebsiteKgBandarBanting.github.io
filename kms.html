PUSAT KEGIATAN MASYARAKAT, KEMAS

KEMAS mission is Empowering Rural Communities Towards Holistic Development


Objective of KEMAS established:
Empowering early childhood education
To provide quality and recognized skills training to enable the rural family economy
Empowering the socio-economics of harmonious, successful and prosperous rural communities
To enhance the development of human
Equipping the mind through education and skills according to current demands
Direct and integrated engagement with other Agencies
Stimulating target groups to brainstorm ideas to plan development programmes.
