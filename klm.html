KOLAM PANCING 69 (JUGRA)

Fishing is undoubtedly a favourite sport and activity to pass the time.
This is due to the fact that fishing not only provides enjoyment but is also more likely to appreciate with all the benefits provided.


Activities at Jugra fishing pond:

The primary activity at a fishing pond is fishing itself.
Grab your fishing rod, bait, and tackle, and try to catch some fish. It's a relaxing and enjoyable way to spend time outdoors.

Bring your camera or smartphone and capture the natural beauty around the fishing pond.
You may be able to get some great shots of wildlife

If you prefer not to keep the fish you catch, practice catch-and-release fishing to ensure the fish population remains healthy.

fishing ponds may organize fishing competitions or tournaments
for participants to showcase their skills and win prizes.

Fishing ponds can be great spots for family outings.
Teach your kids how to fish, play outdoor games, or simply enjoy quality time together.
