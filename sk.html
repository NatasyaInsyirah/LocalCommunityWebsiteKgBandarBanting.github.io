SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN BANDAR

Sekolah Kebangsaan Bandar or its abbreviated name SK Bandar, is a Sekolah Kebangsaan located at Sk Bandar, Jalan Istana, Kampung Bandar.

Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) refers to the primary school and schools that use Bahasa Melayu as the medium of instruction. Main objective is to give education to
children in early age from 7 years old until 12 years old, these schools were established as mainstream schools to foster the unity of Malaysians.

Subject they use to teach:

Bahasa Melayu
English
History
Science
Mathematic
Islamic/Moral
Sports
