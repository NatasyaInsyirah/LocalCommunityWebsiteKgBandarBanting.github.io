THE ROYAL TOMB OF KAMPUNG BANDAR

The Royal Tomb of Kampung Bandar, Jugra,

which is the burial place of Selangor's royal relatives from earlier times until now.

Mother to the Seventh Sultan of Selangor, Sultan Hisamuddin Alam Shah, Cik Hasnah Binti Pilong,was also buried here.
