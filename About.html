WELCOME TO KAMPUNG BANDAR, BANTING!

Kampung Bandar is one of the most beautiful and picturesque towns in Malaysia's state of Selangor. It is located in 42700 Banting, Selangor.

Istana Bandar is one of the interesting spots to visit in this town, which is something that most people are unaware of.


Additionally, Masjid DiRaja Sultan Ala'eddin is one of the popular locations and is frequently used by the neighborhood's residents to conduct Islamic activities.


Even though the town is small and little recognized due to its isolated location, it offers great facilities such as mosque, clinic, and school.

These facilities help the local community to get high-quality education and healthcare,

as well as to strengthen bonds within the community.

But most importantly, everyone here is really friendly.

They will greet you and encourage you to do beautiful things here with them,

such as visit the mosque and enjoy the town's breathtaking surroundings together.
